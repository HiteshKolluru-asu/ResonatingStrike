Project: Resonating Strike
Goal: Build a specialized search tool that can "read" thousands of tournament comments and give you smart answers about what happened.

✅ What is Done
The Data Harvest: You have successfully pulled 1,565 comments from the "First Stand 2026" tournament threads on Reddit.

The Foundation: You have a file (resonating_strike_data.csv) that acts as the "brain" of your project. It contains every reaction, from the first match to the Grand Finals.

🛠️ What We Are Doing Now
Quality Control (The Audit): We are checking how much of that data is actually useful.

The Goal: We want to separate the "Gold" (deep analysis of the games) from the "Gravel" (automated bot posts, "GG" spam, and repetitive jokes).

🚀 The Future Steps
Cleaning the Data: We will run a script to delete the bots and the one-word "spam" comments. This leaves us with a high-quality "Knowledge Base."

Keyword Search (BM25): We teach the tool to find exact names, like "Bin" or specific champions, perfectly.

Meaning Search (Vectors): we teach the tool to understand "vibes." It will learn that someone saying "they played poorly" is the same as someone saying "it was a disaster," even if the words are different.

The AI Chatbot: We connect your smart search to an AI (like a mini-ChatGPT) that uses those specific comments to answer your questions accurately.
